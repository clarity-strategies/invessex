Essex is the UK’s multimodal logistics gateway. Locating at Essex’s multimodal hub can enable logistics businesses to increase supply chain efficiency, reduce costs, emissions and business risks.

Some of the global businesses that have positioned themselves in this optimal location include…

*   **Amazon** – announced a 2m sq ft warehouse at the London Distribution Park at Tilbury last year
*   **UPS** – made their largest non-USA investment in a £120m distribution centre at London Gateway Logistics Park
*   **DP World** – the UK’s largest-ever single-site port investment (£1.5 billion) accommodating the world’s largest container ships

### A viable distribution hub

{% include right-image.html src="//res.cloudinary.com/investessex/image/upload/Ldn_gateway_rail_terminal_400.jpg" width="400" caption="London Gateway" %}

Essex offers unrivalled access to complete multimodal connectivity…

*   4 sea ports, capable of handling all cargo types
*   6 port-linked rail freight terminals, connecting businesses directly to UK, European and Asian markets
*   3 portcentric distribution sites, totalling over 1,000 acres of developable land
*   6 London airports accessible within 90 minutes, including London Heathrow and London Stansted
*   Fast market access by road, via the M25 London orbital motorway ,ro-ro and rail services to key UK and European markets
